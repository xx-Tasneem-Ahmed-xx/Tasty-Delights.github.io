Pink Lemonade




Description

This refreshing Pink Lemonade is naturally pink and perfect for sipping all summer long!
Shout out to Allison on Celebrating sweets


Ingredients
  • ½ cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup diced watermelon
  • 4 cups cold water
Instructions
  1. Add the watermelon, sugar, lemon juice, and 1 cup of water in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Place a fine mesh sieve over a pitcher
  4. pour the mixture through the sieve
  5. Add the remaining water to the pitcher and stir until combined
