Vegetable soup




Description

Let's hug over bowls of steamy vegetable soup with huge hunks of seedy sourdough! Cuz everyone needs a legit soup recipe in their back pocket and this is yours
Shout out to Alexis Joseph on Hummusapien


Ingredients
  • Olive oil
  • Mirepoix
  • Garlic
  • Potatoes
  • Kidney beans
  • Italian seasoning
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Corn
  • Broth
  • Lemon
  • Parsley
Instructions
  1. Make the onions sweat
  2. Add garlic, carrots, celery and potatoes and cook 5 more minutes.
  3. Add Italian seasoning and cook for a minute
  4. Add broth, water, corn, beans, tomatoes, and bay leaves
  5. Bring to a boil. Simmer covered over low heat for 35 minutes, or until veggies are tender
  6. Stir in the parsley and lemon and season to taste
